TOMORROW X TOGETHER

  • TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT), read as "tomorrow by together", is a South Korean K-POP boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment.
  • TXT consists of five members, namely YEONJUN, SOOBIN, BEOMGYU, TAEHYUN, and HUENINGKAI.
  • They debuted on March 4, 2019 with the debut EP "The Dream Chapter: STAR".
  • Currently, they have earned their first No. 1 on the Billboard 200 with the song "Sugar Rush Ride", collaborated with the Jonas Brothers on the song "Do It Like That", and are preparing for their next comeback album: "The Name Chapter: FREEFALL".
