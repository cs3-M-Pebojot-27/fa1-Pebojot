My Favorite Music Artists

AKMU

  • AKMU, or Akdong Musician, is a South Korean musical duo consisting of siblings (brother and sister) Lee Chan-hyuk and Lee Suh-hyun.
  • AKMU debuted under YG Entertainment in April 2014 after winning first place in SBS K-pop Star 2.
  • Their music is under the South Korean Indie/Folk music genre.
  • Their latest release is a song titled "Love Lee"; it is now trending all over the internet, accompanied by a popular dance challenge.
